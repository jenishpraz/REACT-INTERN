Description

A responsive navigation bar that automatically adjusts to different screen sizes. On larger screens (desktop), it is displayed as a horizontal top bar with a logo and navigation links. On smaller screens (mobile), it transforms into a fixed sidebar on the left, while the main content appears on the right, providing a smooth and user-friendly browsing experience across all devices.
